Indonesian Tourist Destinations

Brown Canyon

Brown Canyon is a former land mining in Meteseh, Tembalang, Semarang. This location has become one of the popular locations in Semarang and is considered similar to the Grand Canyon in the United States.

Benoa Beach

Tanjung Benoa is a tourist spot in Bali which is famous for its beaches. This place is also a paradise for water rides such as banana boats, scuba diving, parasailing, rolling donuts, seawalkers, flying fish, snorkeling, etc.

Borobudur Temple

Borobudur Temple Complex is a term used by the World Heritage label of the area of ​​three Buddhist temples in Central Java, Indonesia. The area includes Borobudur, Mendut, and Pawon.

Tupak Sewu Waterfall

Tumpak Sewu Waterfall or also called Coban Sewu is a waterfall with a height of about 120 meters. This waterfall is bordered by Lumajang Regency and Malang Regency, East Java Province.
